Manufacturing Processes

Process for production of edible Cutlery comprises of mixture of edible materials such as starch, agricultural residues, bael fruit and jaggery which are heated under the temperature of 40o C to 70o C then obtained dough is molded under compression molding machine under the pressure of 80 to 170 bar for about 5 to 60 seconds, at a temperature ranging from 150oC to 225o C to form final product and the obtained product is coated with water proof coating material. Finally the produced cutleries are dried and sterilized before packing. The said cutlery degrades in 7 to 30 days. The said cutleries are edible and can be used as feeds to cattle. The materials of the present invention helps to decreases the porosity of the products, thus increases the water holding capacity of the product.
