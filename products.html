Edible spoons

wheat flour

Wheat flour is made through grinding wheat kernels. It is used as the base for edible spoons

Rice

Rice is the second ingridient used for making the spoons and gives the spoons toughness

Ragi

Ragi is the third ingridient which is used and is used to provide calcium to the spoons as they can be a healthy snack.

Milliets

help in the structural intergrity of the spoon making it strong and contains complex carbohydrates
